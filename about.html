Who We Are?

Health Age would be translated to a daycare center for the country's senior citizens. It would be a relaxing hotspot for the elderly, as they spend their mornings alone waiting for their family members to arrive from work or school. This institution provides various facilities that are mentioned on the website. Health Age provides a free service for those elders who come from a poor financial background, but would still like to enjoy the pleasures of old age, can attend for a minimal fees. The Health Age is a start up company by 7 graduates, that aim to help and provide service to the community that is most neglected in the present times. This company was made to assist the elderly in spending time with new friends and achieving better health conditions. This was planned and started of because, the graduates saw their elders being alone, wanting help and some company. Hence Health Age is Dedicated to the grandparents of the 7 graduates.
